Mōchirīī

Motto: “Have a CupCake, Share the Frosting!”
A quiet, cozy corner where kind hearts roam, Mōchirīī welcomes all to a cupcake home. No pressure, no hurry, just sweetness to share, Be gentle, be warm, show kindness & care. 30-day Cupcake nap perfectly fine, Just leave us a note, your seat will still shine.

Ranks

From Soft Wind to Vice Leader, every step is earned through presence, kindness, and steady effort.

Soft Wind

New arrival. Learn the culture, meet the people, settle in. Courtesy and a minimum of 1k guild activity are the only requirements.

Mist Petal

Finding footing. Begins engaging in chat and activities. Shows patience, respect, and a willingness to learn.

Initiate

Time based. Member for at least one full month. The only rank granted purely by time.

Mochi Blossom

Active member. Participates, groups when possible, and adds to the community’s daily rhythm with a steady, friendly presence.

Veteran

Consistency. Member for at least three months. Active on most days and reliably helpful. No leadership duties required.

Elite

Execution helper. Frequently helps with content and questions. Known for accurate guidance, reliability, and calm conduct.

Petal Keeper

Culture steward. Welcomes new members, reinforces guild norms, de-escalates minor issues, and flags concerns early to officers.

Dharmapala

Officer. Leads instances and progression content. Teaches systems, mentors members, and prevents drama.

Hall Leader

Council. Strategic council that oversees officers, resolves escalated disputes, and protects long-term guild stability.

Vice Leader

Highest honor. Top responsibility and trust. Represents Mōchirīī externally, manages diplomacy, and ensures continuity of leadership.

Rules / Charter Highlights

Clear and kind, gently aligned. Harmony held, not tightly confined.

Kindness • Respect • Good manners
  • Lead with kindness: speak with care, be calm & true, soft words often carry farther than loud ones do.
  • Help when you can: teach with grace, guide without pride, we grow much faster when we walk side by side.
  • Keep troubles small: whisper first, don’t let them grow, most storms pass quickly when handled slow.
  • Represent Mōchirīī well: your steps reflect the hall, how you act outside echoes back to us all.
  • No rank excuses bad behavior: titles may rise, but kindness must stay, respect is the path that never decays.
  • Be steady & true: little by little, day after day, quiet effort is how legends are made.

Promotions

Ranks aren’t chased, they quietly grow when care is shown and hearts stay slow, the path unfolds as kindness flows.

Culture

Community

    Rules / Charter Highlights

    Clear and kind, gently aligned. Harmony held, not tightly confined.

    Kindness • Respect • Good manners
    • Lead with kindness: speak with care, be calm & true, soft words often carry farther than loud ones do.
    • Help when you can: teach with grace, guide without pride, we grow much faster when we walk side by side.
    • Keep troubles small: whisper first, don’t let them grow, most storms pass quickly when handled slow.
    • Represent Mōchirīī well: your steps reflect the hall, how you act outside echoes back to us all.
    • No rank excuses bad behavior: titles may rise, but kindness must stay, respect is the path that never decays.
    • Be steady & true: little by little, day after day, quiet effort is how legends are made.

    Ranks aren’t chased, they quietly grow when care is shown and hearts stay slow, the path unfolds as kindness flows.

    Rules / Charter Highlights

    Rules / Charter Highlights

    Clear and kind, gently aligned. Harmony held, not tightly confined.

    Kindness • Respect • Good manners
    • Lead with kindness: speak with care, be calm & true, soft words often carry farther than loud ones do.
    • Help when you can: teach with grace, guide without pride, we grow much faster when we walk side by side.
    • Keep troubles small: whisper first, don’t let them grow, most storms pass quickly when handled slow.
    • Represent Mōchirīī well: your steps reflect the hall, how you act outside echoes back to us all.
    • No rank excuses bad behavior: titles may rise, but kindness must stay, respect is the path that never decays.
    • Be steady & true: little by little, day after day, quiet effort is how legends are made.
